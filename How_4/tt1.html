Instructions and Rules

* Player Roles: Two players compete, one symbolizing 'X' and the other representing 'O'.

* The Core Objective: The primary aim is lining up three identical markers (either Xs or Os) in a sequence - whether it's vertical, horizontal, or diagonal.

* Game Progression: Players place their symbols on any vacant spot. Conventionally, 'X' leads the game.

* Order of play: Players take turns in a set sequence, ensuring no one skips their move.

* The Victory Condition: A player triumphs by successfully placing three of their markers in an unbroken line.

* Draw Scenario: If all board cells are occupied without either player achieving the three-marker alignment, it's deemed a draw or a 'catâ€™s game'.
